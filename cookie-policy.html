Cookie Policy

Introduction

When accessing our services, with your consent we may store information on your device. This information is referred to as “cookies”, which are small text files containing letters and numbers for recording your preferences. Cookies are stored on your device when you use our services while visiting our websites and online pages.

We also use local shared objects or “flash cookies”. Flash cookies are similar to browser cookies and allow us to remember your visits across our sites. Neither cookies nor flash cookies can be used to access your device or use information on your computer. We use these methods solely for monitoring purposes.

Cookies help us monitor traffic to our site, improve our services, make it easier for you to access them, and increase your engagement with our offerings. We also use flash cookies and other cookies to help us show you more relevant and targeted advertisements.

Cookies Placed on Your Device

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are used to allow users to navigate the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas or making financial transactions. Without these cookies, you would not be able to use our websites efficiently.

Important and Necessary Cookies

These cookies hold information collected during registration and allow us to recognize you as a customer, providing you with the services you require. We may also use this data to better understand your online interests and preferences and to continuously enhance your experience on our platforms.

Registration Process Cookies

We use cookies to collect information for visitors to our website. Our servers use three different types of cookies:

  • Session-based cookies: These cookies are allocated to your computer only for the duration of your visit. They help you navigate our website faster and, if you are a registered customer, allow us to provide more relevant information. These cookies expire automatically when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your computer for a set period, depending on the cookie settings. Flash cookies are also persistent.
  • Analytical cookies: These cookies allow us to recognize and count the number of visitors to our site and see how visitors use our content and services, helping us improve the website’s functionality.

You always have the choice to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to manage your cookie files. Options include deleting, blocking, or allowing all cookies, blocking third-party cookies, or using a private browsing mode that doesn't store data locally.

Managing Cookies

You can use your web browser to:

  • Delete all cookies;
  • Block all cookies;
  • Allow all cookies;
  • Block third-party cookies;
  • Clear all cookies when the browser is closed;
  • Open a private browsing/incognito session to browse without storing data locally;
  • Install add-ons and plug-ins to extend browser options.

For more information about managing cookies, please refer to the following links:

  • Information about cookies in Internet Explorer
  • Information about cookies in Chrome
  • Information about cookies in Firefox
  • Information about cookies in Safari
  • Information about cookies in Opera

Flash Cookies

You can modify your Flash Player settings to prevent the use of flash cookies. The settings manager of your Flash Player allows you to manage your preferences.

Please note, if you choose to decline all cookies in your browser, you may not be able to use some features and services on our websites, and some services may not work correctly (for example, saving your chosen interface language).
